#100DaysOfCode Challenge

When I joined Treehouse in May 2020 they were promoting this #100DaysOfCode challenge which I had never heard about. After doing some research I decided to join with the challenge. The basic idea is to code 100 days straight and let others know about your progress on social media. I chose Twitter to be that channel I would use to tweet about my progress. I did code and tweeted every day and also tagged Treehouse in my tweets so they could cheer me on.

It was truly an amazing experience and showed me how much you can achieve by being consistent. Consistency is a powerful tool if you wish to be really good at something. After completing the challenge (on August 31th) I was awarded with a nice badge from Treehouse. I decided to go for a 2nd round of #100DaysOfCode in September and completed it on December 18th.

After completing my first round of the #100DaysOfCode challenge I reflected back on the experience and posted some thoughts on this. You can read about it here.
